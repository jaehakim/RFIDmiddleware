Key Design Decisions

Dark Theme

NOC/Monitoring Center style. Reduces eye strain during extended monitoring. Status colors have higher contrast on dark backgrounds. Glow effects are only possible on dark.

Sidebar Navigation

Persistent status group display - always visible regardless of scroll. Click to filter. Search always accessible. Quick actions one click away.

Matrix View

Ultra-compact 42px cells show all 50 readers without scrolling. Color-coded backgrounds for instant status recognition. Hover for details. Best for "at a glance" monitoring.

Grouped View

Readers organized by status (Reading > Connected > Error > Offline). Priority ordering - problems visible first. Compact chip layout within each group.

Status Ring Animation

Center ring element pulses for active states (reading/error). Glow effects highlight live readers. Static ring for stable states. Disconnected readers are dimmed (opacity).

Top Stats Bar

Large numeric KPIs always visible: Total / Online / Reading / Error. Instant situational awareness without scanning individual cards.

Swing Implementation

  • Dark theme: UIManager.put() for global dark Look & Feel, or custom paint in each component
  • Sidebar: JSplitPane with fixed left panel (220px), or BorderLayout WEST
  • Glow effects: Graphics2D with RadialGradientPaint or semi-transparent fillOval layers
  • Matrix view: GridLayout with fixed-size cells, each a custom painted JPanel
  • Grouped view: Vertical BoxLayout with collapsible sections (JPanel + FlowLayout per group)
  • Animations: javax.swing.Timer for pulse/blink (already implemented for blinkTimer)
  • View switching: CardLayout on the main content panel
